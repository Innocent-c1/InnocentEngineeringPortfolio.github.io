IEEE Membership

After being an IEEE volunteer for over a year, I have been accepted as a full member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE-FUTO Chapter).

The IEEE is a professional association for electronic engineering and electrical engineering (and associated disciplines) with its corporate office in New York City and its operations center in Piscataway, New Jersey. It was formed in 1963 from the amalgamation of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Radio Engineers. The IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
